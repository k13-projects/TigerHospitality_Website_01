**Expected Impact**: 2-4 weeks for improved crawling and indexing

### 2. Claim/Optimize Google Business Profile
**Why**: This is critical for local search and appearing in Google Maps
**Steps**:
1. Go to https://business.google.com
2. Search for existing profiles for:
   - Tiger Hospitality Group
   - Lobster Lab
   - Cosmos
   - La Vida
   - Good Enough
   - Egg n Out
3. Claim each location (if not already claimed)
4. Verify ownership (phone, postcard, or email)
5. Complete ALL profile fields:
   - Business name, category, description
   - Address, phone, website URL
   - Hours of operation
   - Menu links
   - Photos (at least 10 per location)
   - Regular posts/updates

**Expected Impact**: 1-2 weeks for local search visibility

**Strategies**:
- **Local Directories**:

  - TripAdvisor
  - OpenTable / Resy
  - San Diego tourism websites
  - Local chamber of commerce

- **Press & Media**:
  - Submit press releases to San Diego news outlets
  - Reach out to food bloggers
  - Contact San Diego Magazine, Eater San Diego

- **Partnerships**:
  - Food delivery platforms (DoorDash, Uber Eats)
  - Local event partnerships
  - Cross-promotion with other San Diego businesses

**Expected Impact**: 3-6 months for significant improvement
