OUR STORY

Crafting Exceptional Experiences Since 2021

TIGER HOSPITALITY emerged from a simple vision: to elevate San Diego's culinary landscape through innovative concepts that celebrate both tradition and innovation. What started as a single restaurant has grown into a collection of unique dining experiences, each with its own story to tell.

Our commitment goes beyond exceptional food and service. We believe in creating spaces where memories are made, where the community gathers, and where every detail contributes to an unforgettable experience. From our carefully curated menus to our thoughtfully designed interiors, every aspect reflects our dedication to excellence.
